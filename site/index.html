What is DRL Coin?

DRL is a community-driven meme coin for the Daegu Rugby Legends. It celebrates the team spirit, funds fun on-chain experiments, and gives fans a token to rally around.

Token Info

  • Symbol: DRL
  • Decimals: 18
  • Initial Supply: 1,000,000 DRL
  • Standard: ERC-20 style (Solidity 0.8.x)
  • Contract: 0xYOUR_CONTRACT_ADDRESS

Utility

  • Team perks & event rewards
  • Community votes & challenges
  • Future NFTs / merch experiments

How to Get DRL

  1. Add the network (your RPC / Chain ID) to MetaMask.
  2. Add the DRL token by contract address.
  3. Swap on our DEX pool (coming soon) or claim from team drops.

DRL is a meme coin for community fun. It has no intrinsic value and is not financial advice.
